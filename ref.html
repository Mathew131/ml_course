Current SF Startup:

Think of Susie as the dating app that shows you the future — literally. Using AI, it creates images of what your potential kids might look like at different ages. So instead of just swiping on a photo, you’re swiping on the story of what could come next. It’s all about helping you connect on a deeper level and imagine the future together, before you even meet.

Not your generic dating app

You will swipe based on your potential baby. Susie takes user-submitted photos, then produces images showing the offspring as an infant, as a toddler, as a teen, and as an adult. It’s a predictive window into compatibility that helps you see the life you could create with someone, before the first date.

Key Features

  1. AI-generated baby predictions using a model at different life stages
  2. Personality type predictions using Myers-Briggs (MBTI) compatibility
  3. Premium filters for height, eye color, and hair color predictions

Technology Stack: Built with Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage), AI model integration, and responsive mobile-first design.

Mission: To empower romantic decision-making using AI-generated projections of future offspring, blending science, aesthetics, and personality prediction into one cohesive dating experience.

“Whatever your mind can conceive and believe, it can achieve” – Napoleon Hill

Dre’s journey has been a testament to his resilience and determination. Half of his success can be attributed to fortuitous circumstances, while the other half was driven by his unwavering motivation. His path started when he made the difficult decision to drop out of the University of Kansas and venture to Florida with only $800 in his pocket and a 91 Acura Integra.

In a stroke of luck, Dre secured a position as a General Manager at an iPhone repair shop within just three months of his arrival. It was at this point that everything seemed to fall into place. Despite receiving minimal assistance from the company’s administration, Dre managed to propel the store’s sales volume by an impressive 33%. It was during this time that he encountered his first mentor, who guided him towards personal development by recommending insightful books. One particular book, ‘Think and Grow Rich’ by Napoleon Hill, taught Dre the invaluable lesson of maintaining a positive mindset.

Armed with this newfound positivity, Dre resolved to leave the retail industry behind. His determination paid off as he secured an internship at a Digital Marketing Agency in Jacksonville, Florida, just six months later. Within a year, the agency’s owner recognized Dre’s talent and urged him to leave his full-time job to work for her. This multimedia Digital Marketing Agency became a pivotal learning ground for Dre, where he acquired various skills such as website development, content creation, and data analysis.

Through this transformative experience, Dre’s true passion for data began to emerge. It opened his eyes to the possibilities and potential within the field.
